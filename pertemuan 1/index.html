History of Lawrence Hydropower

In 1874 Lawrence Kansas residents had burned all the region's wood, and fossil fuels were not available.
Orlando Darling built a dam across the kansas river north of town and strung mechanical cables from a water wheel to power locate industries.
Justin Bowersock bought the operation, naming it "The Bowersock Mills & Power Company"(Bowersock). Bowersock's slogan is:

Producing clean, renewable hydropower since 1874
